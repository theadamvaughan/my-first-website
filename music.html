Music

Another interest of mine is music and DJing. Prior to learning how to become a software engineer, I hosted a weekly radio show on www.codesouth.fm called "The Weekend Has Landed", which I did for about 4 years. The shows name was inspired by a speech in the 1999 film, "Human Traffic", and that it was every Friday from 6 to 8pm which is about the time people finish their working week.

I was also lucky enough to be a resident at the Honey Club in Brighton. During my 2 years there I warmed up for various stars such as Judge Jules, Danny Howard, Chuckie and Norman Jay MBE.

In 2013 I did a season in Ibiza selling tickets around San Antoinio for Ushuaia. Ushuaia is an open-air club based in Playa Den Bossa and has regularly been placed amongst the best nightclubs in the world. During my time on the white isle I played at various bars, but the main highlight was playing at Eden.

I have selected s few shows and mixes from my mixcloud page below. If you want to hear more, head over to www.mixcloud.com/TheWeekendHasLandedOfficial

The Last EVER Weekend Has Landed - 24 November 2017

After 4 years of playing on Codesouth, it was time to hang up my headphones. Here's my last ever show.

I was lucky enough to be joined by turntable wizard, Krafty Kuts. A DJ renowned and respected worldwide for his highly technical turntablism, meticulously crafted DJ sets and party-punching productions. This mix hit number 42 in the breaks chart.

The Weekend Has Landed Constant Cirlces Special with Just Her - 02 June 2017

In this episode, I was joined by Constant Circles head honcho and one half of Him_Self_Her; Just Her.

"It's my brithday and i'll mix if I want to" I decided to to do a mix featuring tunes that went down well at parties. This hit number 27 in the funky chart and 50th in the classics chart. Strong language is used so listener discretion is advised.

Broom Cupboard Beats - 15 January 2013
