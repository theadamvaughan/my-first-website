What's all this racket?

Every week, Adam brings you The Weekend Has Landed live every Friday from 6-8pm on www.codesouth.fm

A regular on the Brighton music scene, Adam was a resident at the former Honey Club (now Shooshh!) and has DJ'd at numbers places in the UK including Pacha in London as well as at Hush, Viva and Eden in Ibiza.

With special guest DJs showcasing the best in dance music, if you like your vocal funky house with a groovy bass line, you'll love the Weekend Has Landed!
