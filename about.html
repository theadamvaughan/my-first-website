About This Website

As I wanted a change of career I have embarked on a course to learn how to code using different programming languages. I am hoping that I gain sufficient knowledge to achieve my the goal of becoming a software engineer.

Under the guidance of Danny Smith I have designed this website from scratch using HTML and CSS. Why not show Danny some love and visit his website at danny.is

As part of my journey I have also used other tools to build websites such as wix.com and wordpress. I hope to understand how both these work and share my experiences as my journey continues.

I have included pages for other interests I have. I hope to expand the website and add additional pages as I learn new and better ways to code.

You can see the full evolution of this website via my GitHub page at theadamvaughan.
