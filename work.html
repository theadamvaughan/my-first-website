Websites I have built

Day2Day Flooring

Published on 24 January 2018

A friend of mine was looking for a website to promote his carpet business.

After understanding what specifications he wanted for his site, I realised that providing him with a website that suited his needs was slightly beyond my skill level at that point.

I had purchased my web address at this point and the free hosting service I used had the option of using work press so I thought I would give it a go.

I didn’t find the sites wordpress plugin very user friendly and moved over to wix.com.

Over the course of a weekend I was able to design his website that he was happy with. We had to sit down and go through the small detail and a bit of user acceptance testing to ensure everything worked.

So, if you’re based near Brighton and need a new carpet, laminate floor or artificial grass, why not head over to www.day2dayflooring.co.uk.
